Attractions

Kalilangan Cold Spring

The crystal-clear waters of this spring are a sight to behold, inviting visitors to immerse themselves in its tranquil beauty.

Mindanao Civic Center

Mindanao Civic Center (MCC) is a multimillion sports complex located in the town of Tubod—the capital town of Lanao del Norte.

Dapit Alim Shrine

Dapit Alim is one of the famous Shrine in Tubod Lanao del Norte. Dapit-Alim is a good place to relax specially if you have a problem.

Panguil Bay Bridge

Panguil Bay Bridge is an under construction 3.77 km (2.34 mi) bridge which will connect Tangub, Misamis Occidental and Tubod, Lanao del Norte.

Mabugnaw Spring

Located at P-1 Tubaran, Tubod Lanao del Norte
